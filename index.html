FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from just asking ChatGPT?+
ChatGPT gives you a generic answer based on your job title. CareerShield builds a structured profile from your specific tasks, cross-references it with real-time market data, and generates a personalized plan that updates monthly.
How accurate is the risk assessment?+
We combine data from Tufts University's AI Jobs Risk Index, the WEF Future of Jobs Report, Anthropic's labor market research, and live job posting trends. The more specific you are about your tasks, the more accurate your result. Our models update monthly as new research is published.
What if I work in a specialized or niche field?+
That's exactly where we shine. Generic tools only analyze broad job categories. Because you describe your actual work, we can differentiate between a junior attorney doing document review (very high risk) and a senior attorney handling complex litigation strategy (lower risk) — even though they share the same title.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no tricks. Cancel from your account in two clicks. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data is always yours — export everything at any time.
Is my career data private?+
Your data is never sold, shared, or used to train AI models. It's stored encrypted and only used to generate your personal analysis. No ads — ever.
